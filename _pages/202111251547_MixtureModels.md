# Mixture Models

Key insight is that we usually are summing **probability densities** not the random variables themselves.

General setup:
$$
p(x) = \sum_k^K \pi_k f_k(x) \\
\sum_k \pi_k = 1 \quad \pi_k \geq 0
$$
