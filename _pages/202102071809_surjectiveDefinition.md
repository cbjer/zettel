# Surjective Definition

A [linear map](202102071416_linearMapDefinition) $T : V \rightarrow W$ is **surjective** if 
its [range](202102071800_rangeDefinition) equals $W$.

In other words:
$$
\forall w \in W, \,  \exists v \in V : T(v) = w
$$
