# $T v$ Orthogonal to $v$

When looking at a complex [inner product space](202102141708_innerProductSpace) with $T$ an [operator](202102082104_operatorDefinition).

If we have $\langle T v , v \rangle = 0$ for all $v \in V$, then $\implies T = 0$
