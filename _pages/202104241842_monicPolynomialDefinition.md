# Monic Polynomial Definition

A **monic polynomial** is a polynomial whose highest degree coefficient equals 1.

Eg:
$$
x^3 + 3 x^2 - 7 \\
x^4 + 1 \\
x^7 - 5x^2
$$
are all monic polynomials.
