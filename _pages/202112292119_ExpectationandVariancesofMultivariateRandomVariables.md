# Expectation and Variances of Multivariate Random Variables

$$
E(\mathbf{a}^T X) = \mathbf{a}^T E(X) = \mathbf{a}^T \mathbf{\mu} \\
\textrm{Var}(\mathbf{a}^T X) = \mathbf{a}^T \Sigma \mathbf{a}
$$
