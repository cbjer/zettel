# Vector Calculus Results

$$
\begin{aligned}
\frac{\partial}{\partial \mathbf{x}} \mathbf{x}^T \mathbf{a} &= \frac{\partial}{\partial \mathbf{x}} \mathbf{a}^T \mathbf{x} = \mathbf{a} \\
\frac{\partial}{\partial \mathbf{x}} (\mathbf{x}^T A \mathbf{x}) &= (A + A^T) \mathbf{x}
\end{aligned}
$$
