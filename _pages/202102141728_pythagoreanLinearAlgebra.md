# Pythagorean Theorem Linear Algebra

If $u$ and $v$ are [orthogonal](202102141725_orthogonalDefinition) in $V$, then:
$$
\Vert u + v \Vert^2 = \Vert u \Vert^2 + \Vert v \Vert^2
$$
