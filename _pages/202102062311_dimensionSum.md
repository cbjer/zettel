# Dimension of Sum

If we have [subspaces](202102061429_subspaceDefinition) $U_1$ and $U_2$ of $V$ then 
$$
\mathrm{dim}(U_1 + U_2) = \mathrm{dim}(U_1) + \mathrm{dim}(U_2) - \mathrm{dim}(U_1 \cap U_2)
$$
