# Confidence Interval

A $1 - \alpha$ **confidence interval** for a parameter $\theta$ is an interval $C_n = (a,b)$ where both $a$ and $b$ are functions of the data, such that

$$
P_{\theta} (\theta \in C_n) \geq 1 - \alpha \quad \forall \theta \in \Theta
$$
