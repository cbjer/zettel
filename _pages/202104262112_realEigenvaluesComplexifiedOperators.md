# Real Eigenvalues of Complexified Operator

Say we have $\lambda \in \mathbf{R}$, then $\lambda$ is an [eigenvalue](202102120912_eigenvalueDefinition) of
$T_C$ if and only if $\lambda$ is an eigenvalue of $T$.
