# Basic Properties of the Norm

If we have a vector $v \in V$, then the following are properties of the [norm](202102141717_normDefinition)
- $\Vert v \Vert = 0 \iff v = 0$
- $\Vert \lambda v \Vert = \vert \lambda \vert \Vert v \Vert $
