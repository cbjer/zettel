# Markov Property for States

A state $S_t$ is Markov if and only if

$$
P ( S_{t+1} | S_1, S_2, \dots, S_t) = P ( S_{t+1} | S_t )
$$

Intuitively we say the future is independent of the past, conditional on the present.
