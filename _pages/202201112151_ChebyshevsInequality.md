# Chebyshev's Inequality

$$
P(\vert X - \mu \vert \geq t) \leq \frac{\sigma^2}{t^2}
$$ 

and 

$$
P(\vert Z \vert \geq k) \leq \frac{1}{k^2} \\
Z = (X - \mu) / \sigma
$$
