# Division Algorithm for Polynomials

If we have polynomials $p, s \in \mathcal{P}(\mathbf{F})$ with $s \neq 0$, then there exists unique polynomials 
$q, r \in \mathcal{P}(\mathbf{F})$ such that 
$$
p = sq + r
$$

with $\mathrm{deg} \, r < \mathrm{deg} \, s$
